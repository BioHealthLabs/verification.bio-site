How data, evidence and affiliate relationships are verified across the ronarn network.

Evidence Integrity · Data Validation · Ranking Verification

Independent verification standards for data, rankings and product claims.

Verification.bio defines how factual content, product data, ingredient science, affiliate relationships and ranking logic are confirmed before being published on any ronarn AS educational or comparison domain.

  • Verification of scientific references & ingredient evidence.
  • Verification of product data (strength, formula, purpose).
  • Verification of ranking logic and editorial scoring.
  • Verification of affiliate neutrality & commercial independence.

Works alongside Standards.bio, Guides.bio and Transparency.bio.

1. Scientific & Evidence Verification

All ingredient-based claims must be verified through:

  • peer-reviewed studies
  • dermatology consensus statements
  • ingredient monographs from recognised bodies
  • reputable medical databases

Only widely accepted, high-quality evidence is used. Conflicting evidence must be disclosed.

2. Product Data Verification

Before any product appears in a comparison table, the following must be verified:

  • INCI ingredient list (cross-checked with manufacturer sources)
  • active ingredient concentration
  • intended use (serum, cleanser, treatment, etc.)
  • packaging size for price/ML calculations
  • rating consistency across retailers & regions

3. Ranking Verification & Editorial Integrity

Rankings are editorial and cannot be altered by sponsorship.

  • Each ranking must have a documented scoring rationale.
  • Every score must map to ingredient strength, usability, and formula quality.
  • Affiliate partnerships must not influence ranking order.

Any ranking adjustments require updated verification notes and date stamps.

4. Affiliate Verification (Neutrality Check)

To ensure commercial transparency:

  • affiliate links must be labelled “sponsored” or “affiliate”
  • affiliate income must not change comparison order
  • affiliate partners must be disclosed at Disclosure.bio

Verification.bio certifies compliance across all ronarn-linked domains.

5. Data Freshness & Update Verification

Product & ingredient data must be reviewed on a rolling schedule:

  • ingredient science → every 12–18 months
  • product availability → quarterly
  • affiliate relationships → monthly
  • ranking logic → per update

6. Relation to the Governance Framework

Verification.bio is one of the “core governance pillars” alongside:

  • Transparency.bio
  • Disclosure.bio
  • Policies.bio
  • Standards.bio
  • Audits.bio

Together, these ensure trustworthiness, accuracy and editorial independence across the full network.
