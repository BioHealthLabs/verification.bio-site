Transparent publisher identity and governance for the entire network.

  • Publisher identity & legal ownership (ronarn AS).

1. Publisher Identity

All comparison and educational websites in the ronarn network are published by ronarn AS, an independent digital-health and app development company based in Norway.

  • No external ownership
  • No sponsorship influence
  • No paid placement
  • No brand-funded editorial content

ronarn AS defines the governance, ethics and independence rules applied to all domains.

2. Editorial Structure

All content follows a clear editorial workflow:

  • research & evidence collection
  • ingredient analysis & scientific review
  • product data verification
  • editorial scoring logic
  • final approval

The workflow ensures that no commercial partner can influence rankings or content.

3. Governance Principles

Organization.bio outlines the rules that apply across all ronarn-published domains:

  • independence of rankings and recommendations
  • strict evidence standards for all claims
  • transparent affiliate disclosures
  • regular content auditing and verification
  • no paid content influencing scoring

These principles ensure user trust and prevent commercial bias.

4. Domains Published Under This Framework

The governance and identity defined here apply to all comparison and educational sites including:

  • Genectics.com – DNA testing
  • Retynol.com – retinol comparisons
  • Niacinimide.com – niacinamide insights
  • Hyaluronic.com – HA product reviews
  • AcneHeals.com – acne treatment analysis
  • Hundreds of specialised .bio educational domains
